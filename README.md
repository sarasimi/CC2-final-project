# Description of the project
This is a sonic experiment, combining multiple types of synthesis to emulate sound.
An IR proximity sensor controls the rhythm of the sound and the laptop webcam controls the "melody".

It requires the participation of two people, that are free to explore it until they feel like they've reached a nice-sounding result.
There are no rules of how it needs to be played, except for intuition! :)

## How to play it
The users need to have MAX MSP installed on their laptops.
One of them needs to trigger the IR proximity sensor, by moving closer to and further away from the sensor. 
The other one needs to have their webcam on and trigger the sound by moving closer to and further away from the camera.
